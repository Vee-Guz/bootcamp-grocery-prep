Hot Chocolate

Don't let the grandma intimidate you.


Serving

1

Ingredients

  • 1 abuelita hot chocolate tablet
  • about 3 cups of milk (i don't measure)
  • 2 cinammon sticks

Steps

  1. fill a pot half way with milk
  2. add the abuelita tablet to milk (don't worry i don't think it has to be boiling)
  3. add them cinammon sticks
  4. stir ocassionally (you want that tablet to dissolve)
  5. serve :)
