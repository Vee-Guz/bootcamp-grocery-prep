Ceviche

This is fishy


Serving

1

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of fish
  • 5 lemons
  • 0.5 white onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • cilantro (finely chopped)

Steps

  1. put fish in large bowl
  2. add the lemon juice into the large bowl (the lemon will cook our raw fish, so you can leave this overnight covered with plastic wrap
  3. now that fish is cooked, add rest of ingedients
  4. serve on top of a tostada (or an a plate with chips because tostada gets soggy) with avacado and valentina
  5. enjoy :)
