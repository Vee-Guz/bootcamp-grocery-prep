Quesadilla

Cheese Cheese Cheese Cheese Cheese Meat Cheese Cheese

Ingredients

  • buy some meat (bistec)
  • cook meat and chop into small squares
  • tortilla (corn or flour; flour = bigger quesadilla)
  • cheese...........

Steps

  1. cook meat and chop into small squares
  2. get two tortillas of your choice. place one on comal
  3. add cheese and meat on top of tortilla
  4. place other tortilla on top
  5. flip the tortilla to your liking. enjoy :)
